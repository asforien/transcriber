Tone Transcription Experiment


Description:

This is an online perceptual study to observe Cantonese tone recognition accuracy in both speakers and non-speakers of Cantonese. The experiment is estimated to take no longer than half an hour.

Requirements:

  1. You are above 21 years of age if you are a Singaporean, or above 18 years of age otherwise
  2. You are using a browser other than Internet Explorer
