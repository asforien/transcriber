Tone Transcription Experiment


Description:

This is an online perceptual study to observe Cantonese tone recognition accuracy in both speakers and non-speakers of Cantonese. The experiment is estimated to take no longer than half an hour. Subjects will be reimbursed SGD $10 for their time. Each person is only eligible for payment once. Participants found to be cheating and/or abusing the system will not be paid.

Requirements:

  1. You are above 21 years of age if you are a Singaporean, or above 18 years of age otherwise
  2. You have access to a computer with a modern browser installed
